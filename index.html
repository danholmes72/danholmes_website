About
Bank-side depth. Vendor-side reach.

I started my career at Lloyds Banking Group - the UK's largest retail bank - where I spent eight years building expertise across fraud analytics, fraud technology, operations and large-scale transformation programmes. That experience gave me a ground-level understanding of how fraud operates from inside a major institution - the data, the model and rule led decisions, the organisational constraints, and what it actually takes to move the needle on performance.

Moving to the vendor side - via LexisNexis Risk Solutions (ThreatMetrix) and then Feedzai - I've spent the past seven years converting that domain knowledge into product, strategy and commercial impact. I've shaped product direction, led product messaging, strategic M&A, and worked directly with some of the world's largest banks to define what best-in-class fraud prevention looks like in practice. I currently lead product planning and strategy at Feedzai, sitting at the intersection of product and market planning, leading a team of 6 direct reports.

My focus remains simple which is to help financial institutions understand where the fraud market is heading - and make sure they are prepared to succeed.
